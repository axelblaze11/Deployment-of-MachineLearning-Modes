Almost 2-8% of people with MDD die by committing suicide. MDD is most severe and lasts a way longer than normal sadness. People usually get treatment via counseling or antidepressant medication. This rate of depression among the people got increased by 18.4% during 2005 to 2015. By 2030, WHO do a prediction that MDD will be the leading cause of disease.
